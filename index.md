## About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University, where I am fortunate to work with Prof. [Ping Zhang](https://www.pingzhang.net) in the AIMed (Artificial Intelligence in Medicine) Lab. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My research focuses on AI in pathology, with a particular interest in **hematopathology**: the study of blood and bone marrow disorders. This is a domain that presents unique challenges distinct from solid tumor pathology.

## Research Interests

- **Artificial Intelligence**
- **AI in Healthcare**
- **Data Mining**
- **Machine Learning**
- **Computational Pathology**
